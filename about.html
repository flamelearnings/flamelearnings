Our Founding

Flame Learnings was established with a vision to make high-quality education accessible to students across the globe. We offer expert-led online tuition for CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, and IB curricula — from LKG to Grade 12 — along with certified Montessori teacher training programs.

Growth & Beyond

Since our inception, Flame Learnings has helped hundreds of students excel academically and teachers enhance their skills. Our commitment to growth ensures that we are always evolving with the latest in educational methods and technology.

Our Teaching Methodology

At Flame Learnings, we conduct all our classes live via Zoom, allowing for real-time interaction between teachers and students. Our sessions are designed to be highly interactive, focusing on conceptual clarity through discussions and problem-solving.

Each student’s progress is monitored through regular assessments and assignments. Dedicated mentors provide one-to-one guidance and timely doubt-clearing sessions to ensure continuous support and academic success.

Certified and Experienced Faculties

Our team consists of highly qualified and certified educators with years of teaching experience in academic and Montessori fields. They are trained to provide personalized guidance, interactive teaching, and timely support to help students and aspiring teachers succeed. Each instructor is carefully selected based on their subject expertise and commitment to student success.

Understanding the Montessori Philosophy

The Montessori method is a child-centered educational approach that encourages self-directed learning, independence, and hands-on exploration. It nurtures the natural curiosity of children through structured activities in a thoughtfully prepared environment.

Our Montessori Teacher Training Course introduces future educators to this philosophy, equipping them with modern, practical techniques for early childhood development.
