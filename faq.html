Frequently Asked Questions

What programs do you offer at Flame Learnings?

Flame Learnings offers high-quality tuition for students in grades 9-12 in various subjects, including Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies. Additionally, we provide a Montessori Teachers Training Course for individuals aspiring to become certified Montessori educators.

How can I enroll in your courses?

To enroll, simply contact us at +91 70252 88288, +91 80868 98888 or +91 85890 88388. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process and help you get started with your classes.

What is the difference between one-to-one and group classes?

One-to-One Classes: These sessions provide personalized attention where the instructor focuses solely on the individual student's learning needs, helping them at their own pace. It's ideal for students who need customized support.

Group Classes: These sessions involve learning with a group of students, offering a collaborative environment. It's great for students who enjoy interaction with peers and prefer a more social learning experience.

How are the classes conducted?

All classes at Flame Learnings are conducted via the Zoom platform, ensuring smooth, real-time interaction between students and teachers.

Who can join the Montessori course?

Anyone interested in teaching young children—whether freshers, homemakers, or career changers—can join the Montessori course. No prior experience is required.

Is there a flexible schedule for classes?

Yes, Flame Learnings offers flexible scheduling options to accommodate students' and trainees' availability and preferences.

Will study materials be provided?

Yes, we provide study materials, notes, and practice worksheets for all subjects and training programs offered at Flame Learnings.
